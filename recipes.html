Directions

Cabbage leaves:

Steam cabbage until softened (but not cooked)

Filling

Cook quinoa (1+ cup) in veggie or chicken stock or add in spices, I added a tablespoon of goat cheese, you can also add vegan almond cheese or regular cheese

Vegetables

I used cauliflower and kale, but use whatever is on hand 1/2 head cauliflower (chopped small) 10 kale leaves (without the stem) chopped into pieces 1 onion (chopped) Sauté until cooked with spices (whatever you like) I used garlic, salt and pepper

Peppers and tomatoes with garlic, olive oil and pepper - you want it to be a bit thinner so it can soak into the cabbage leaves when it cooked

Rolling:

Roll the cabbage leave from the top to the stem and tuck the sides in as you roll (like a burrito) Cook covered for 30 - 45 minutes at 140

Patties
Veggie patties
Prep. time:
15 minutes
Total Time:
2.5 Hours
Serves:
8

Ingridients:

  • 2 cups chopped cabbage (the smaller the better)
  • 2 cups frozen (thawed) spinach (squeeze out excess liquid)
  • 1 1/2 cups riced broccoli
  • 1 leek chopped in halves
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup garbanzo flour (or flour of your choice)
  • Seasoning + Grated Cheese (optional)

Directions

Saute broccoli & spinach in a 1/4 table spoon avocado oil and some seasoning (5 minutes - medium heat) - gets rid of the chill and helps get some flavor into the frozen veggies Put cooked veggies into a bowl Sauté leek and cabbage in 1/4 table spoon avocado oil (add seasoning) (garlic, peppers, chili pepper + others) Combine all cooked veggies in the same bowl with seasoning eggs, 1/2 flour and grated cheese (optional) Mix until combined and add more flour if its not the right consistency

Heat pan on medium heat with 1/2 table spoon oil (if its non stick it doesn’t matter) and then cook on each side for a few minutes or until golden brown and flip.

Egg Sandwich
Prep. time:
????
Total Time:
????
Serves:
????

Ingridients:

  • gluten free bread
    from (Lechem Pomerantz, Gluteria or Blikemach)
  • 11/4 cups of warm water
  • 1 teastspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoos of oil

Directions:

Bread

I use gluten free bread from (Lechem Pomerantz, Gluteria or Blikemach) Toast on both sides in a toaster or in a pan with a small amount of oil or butter (spray works great) **not all oil sprays are created equal, check the ingredients list there should only be 1 (the oil)

Eggs

Spray pan with some oil (2 second spray) Add scrambled eggs and/or egg whites to the pan Use a spatula to move the eggs around the pan once you see the bottom layer has cooked Try no to break the eggs into piece, but rather keep them cooking together.

On a plate:

On a plate: Put toast side by side, spread some dijon mustard on one side of each, then add cheese if desired. Place scrambled eggs on top of one half and close your sandwich!
