Sun-Dried Timato Pizza
Prep. time:
15 minutes
Total Time:
2.5 Hours
Serves:
8

Ingridients:

  • 1 tables sugar
  • 11/4 cups of warm water
  • 1 teastspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoos of oil

Directions

Grab a Siete Tortilla or pre-bought store crust.

Wait, I can eat pizza on the paleo diet?! Sure can! You just have to make sure you skip a dough with wheat and gluten and dairy (dairy-free mozzarella is surprisingly easy to find these days). But vegetables and pepperoni are totally allowed for topping.

Patties
Veggie patties
Prep. time:
15 minutes
Total Time:
2.5 Hours
Serves:
8

Ingridients:

  • 2 cups chopped cabbage (the smaller the better)
  • 2 cups frozen (thawed) spinach (squeeze out excess liquid)
  • 1 1/2 cups riced broccoli
  • 1 leek chopped in halves
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup garbanzo flour (or flour of your choice)
  • Seasoning + Grated Cheese (optional)

Directions

Saute broccoli & spinach in a 1/4 table spoon avocado oil and some seasoning (5 minutes - medium heat) - gets rid of the chill and helps get some flavor into the frozen veggies Put cooked veggies into a bowl Sauté leek and cabbage in 1/4 table spoon avocado oil (add seasoning) (garlic, peppers, chili pepper + others) Combine all cooked veggies in the same bowl with seasoning eggs, 1/2 flour and grated cheese (optional) Mix until combined and add more flour if its not the right consistency

Heat pan on medium heat with 1/2 table spoon oil (if its non stick it doesn’t matter) and then cook on each side for a few minutes or until golden brown and flip.
