"Burrito" Veggie or Protein Bowls

Prep time - minutes
Cook time - minutes
Total - minutes
Base:
  • Quinoa (1/2 cup)
  • or Cauliflower (1 cup riced - 2 cups if florets)
  • or Lentil (1/2 cup)
  • Option: mix 1/4 cup 2 or more
Protein:
  • 1/4 cup Chickpeas
  • 1/4 Beans or 3-4 ounces Meat
Toppings:
  • Avocado (1/4 healthy fat and protein)
  • Corn (1/4 cup)
  • Broccoli
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Bell Peppers

*The nice thing about this is you can make a batch of the base or protein in advance so you can assemble it really quickly
