Spinach Veggie Patties
(Think Trader Joes Spinach Puffs)

Prep time 20 minutes
Cook time 30-40 minutes
Total 60 minutes
Ingredients:
  • 1-2 cups chopped leeks
  • 1 cup chopped mushroom
  • 1 cup chopped Swiss chard or spinach (4-6 cubes)
    (You can use frozen spinach, thaw, squeeze out the excess water and add to the bowl)
  • 4-5 cloves garlic chopped + garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons avocado oil
  • 2 tablespoons flour of choice
  • 2 eggs
  • Avocado or Coconut Oil
Preparation/Cooking
  1. Heat pan with avocado or coconut oil (1 tbps) on medium to low heat
  2. Add the garlic let cook for 3-5 minutes
  3. Add in the leeks and mushrooms cook for 5 minutes
  4. Add in Swish chard and spinach
  5. Season with (seasonings of choice) Garlic + Salt and Pepper
  6. Cook for an additional few minutes or until all veggies have started to reduce together
  7. Add all ingredients to a bowl
  8. Add in Eggs and additional seasoning if necessary or desired
  9. Mix all together - combine into patties
    10. a. I use two spoons to portion out each pattie
  10. Using the same pan - put some additional oil or spray on the bottom (Minimally is great)
  11. Start adding in patties and cook on each side for ~3-5 minutes or until golden brown and molded together

You can add as many types of veggies as you like.

This also works with shredded zucchini just make sure to squeeze out all excess water
