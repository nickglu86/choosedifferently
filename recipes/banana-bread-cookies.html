Peanut butter Banana Bread Cookies

Prep time 10 minutes
Cook time 20-30 minutes
Total 40 minutes
Ingredients:
  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
    (I used @nowfoodsofficial)
  • 1 cup gluten free oat flour
    (I used @bobsredmill)
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter or other nut butter (Look for 1 ingredient, no added sugar or salt)
  • 1tsp baking soda & baking powder
  • Chocolate chips (if desired)

Use a large spoon to portion out the batter into a baking sheet lined with parchment paper

Bake 150c or 300f for ~20 minutes
